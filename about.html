Information Science?

  • A rather new, multidisciplanry major that approaches
    tech from the human perspective

  • Classes ranged from theoritical aspects of technology like the ethics of big data
    where we were taught how to evaluate major privacy and security questions raised by big data, IoT, wearables and other AI-driven systems.
    We covered history of research ethics and considered how ethical frameworks can and should be applied to digital data.

  • To classes such as data base design where we learned how to build a database and query it using the SQL language and mySQL server;
    we also cleaned, wrangled, and analyzed datasets with over 230,000 entries and 12 columns
    using python and openrefine in various data analysis classes throughout the four years.

  • I use the tools I was provided by my major everyday. But more than that, Information Science has taught me how to search for knowledge correctly and effeciently, which in my opinion is the most valuable tool.
